Frequently Asked Questions
  • How long will delivery take?
    Delivery can take from 3 to 7 days, each item will state how long it should take to arrive.

  • What courier do you use?
    We use both Royal Mail and Evri.

  • Do I need an account?
    No, you can check out as a guest, however there are some perks with an account such as being able to keep track of your orders and receiving discount codes every now and then.

  • Is there an option to sign in with Google?
    No, not yet. We will add this as an option alongside normal registration in the future.

  • How can I track my orders?
    You should receive an email with the tracking number as soon as it becomes available.

  • I would like to to cancel an order that I have placed.
    Send us an email here. If the item has not yet been shipped we will cancel your order and send you a full refund.
